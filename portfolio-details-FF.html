Project Overview

During the first week of April, DAE organized a gamejam together with Xbox GameCamp. After forming our groups we had the option to choose between 3 different themes: "Outgrow hunger", "Minesweeper" and "Friend slop". We were also allowed to use multiple themes. Our game had to be finished within two and a half days.

My group consisted of 2 animators, a sound designer, a game designer and 2 developers (me included). We decided to go for the theme "Friend slop", but we still added some minesweeper aspects into it. We also made the decision to work with Unity, since most of our team members were familiar with it.

The game takes place at a birthday party in a world of rats. The players (big rats) have to keep all the children happy by fulfilling their wants. In the party there are multiple stands and a delivery truck, delivering various items. The children will want for these items, it's the player's job to bring these items and the children to the correct stand.

Features (I worked on)

Local Multiplayer

This game can be played with up to 4 players on the same device!

Delivery truck

The delivery truck will bring boxes to the party. These boxes are layed out on a grid. The player can reveal the items by opening the boxes. However, certain boxes hold confetti grenades!

Stands

The player has to bring the items and children to the correct stand. While holding an item the corresponding stand is highlighted.
